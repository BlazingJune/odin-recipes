Pot Stickers

About these little delights

We get these from Trader Joes, because no other brand will compare. Get veggie if you like; the pork ones are even better.

Ingredients

  • trader joe's potstickers
  • peanut oil
  • water

Steps

  1. Heat the oil on the stove
  2. Put the potstickers on high heat for 5 minutes. This is your only chance to get a sear.
  3. Reduce heat, cover, and steam with a bit of water.
