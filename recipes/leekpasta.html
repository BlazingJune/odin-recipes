Leek Pasta

About this pasta

This pasta comes from a Real Simple catalogue my mother found when she had to feed five of us. I didn't know that at the time, so to me it was nothing but an excellent meal where I didn't have to eat around the red sauce.

Ingredients

  • fettucine
  • leeks
  • white wine
  • heavy cream
  • arugula

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Throw the noodles together with the rest of the ingredients.
  3. That's it.
