Artichokes with Hollandaise

What are these weird treats?

It's a bizarre birthday tradition. If you are a Sullivan, you most likely will have boiled artichokes with extremely delicious hollandaise sauce as some element of your birthday dinner. I don't make the rules.

Ingredients

  • artichokes
  • eggs
  • butter

Steps

  1. Boil the artichokes.
  2. Blend the eggs and butter in the perfect way to get a creamy hollandaise sauce.
  3. To eat, peel the artichoke leaf by leaf, dip the bottom end into the sauce and scrape the top with your teeth.
